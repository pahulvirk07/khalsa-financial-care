Welcome to Khalsa Financial Care!

Our Mission

Empowering new Punjabi immigrants with the financial knowledge to thrive in America through accessible and culturally relevant education.

Our Story

Founded by Pahul Virk and Amrit Mann, we understand the unique challenges faced by immigrant families, inspiring us to help others navigate their financial journeys confidently.

Our Vision

We envision a community where every new immigrant can make informed financial decisions, bridging the knowledge gap and fostering independence.

Our Approach

  • Workshops: Interactive sessions tailored to community needs.
  • Resources: Easy-to-understand materials for ongoing support.
  • One-on-One Guidance: Personalized support for unique financial situations.
